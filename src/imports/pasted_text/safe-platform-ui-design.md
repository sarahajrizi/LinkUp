
Design a modern, clean, high-trust digital health platform UI for a product called “Safe”.
SAFE is a proactive child health intelligence and Home Visiting management platform designed for Kosovo’s healthcare system. The platform digitizes child health records, vaccination tracking, developmental milestones, and Home Visiting nurse workflows while enabling real-time preventive care, AI-powered risk alerts, and parent-healthcare coordination.
The product combines:
•	a mobile-first NurseApp for field nurses,
•	a FamilyPortal for parents,
•	and a real-time AdminDashboard for municipalities and Ministry of Health officials.
Create the frontend design in a polished gov-tech + health-tech SaaS style with a professional, trustworthy, modern, and institutional look.
The UI should communicate:
•	trust,
•	healthcare reliability,
•	prevention,
•	care coordination,
•	child safety,
•	intelligent public health infrastructure,
•	and modern digital governance.
Avoid childish healthcare aesthetics or cartoon medical styles.
The design should feel like:
•	a next-generation UNICEF / WHO / government digital health platform,
•	modern,
•	premium,
•	highly structured,
•	emotionally reassuring,
•	and technologically advanced.
Visual Direction:

•	Soft cards and panels

•	Calm healthcare atmosphere
•	White and neutral backgrounds
•	Blue and indigo as primary accents
•	Emerald/green for healthy/completed states

•	Responsive mobile-first design
